NPCs
There are a small handful of NPCs in Bus Simulator that you can interact with, most of them being seen in events. Majority of the NPCs can be interacted with, and have small conversations with them, while others have a small quest behind them, for an item in return.

Majority of the NPCs can get annoyed if you spam the Airhorn around them, and some will even threaten you if you don't stop annoying them. All of the NPCs also has a chance of appearing in events like Passengers and No Dough.
